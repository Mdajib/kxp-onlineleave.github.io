COMPASSIONATE LEAVE

In  the  event  of  a  death  of  members  of  employees  immediate family (spouse, children, father ,mother, father and mother in laws). Leave must be supported by documentary proofs.


EMERGENCY LEAVE

On the occurrence of flood, fire or other disaster which affects the property of the employees. Leave must be supported by documentary proofs.



SICK AND HOSPITALISATION LEAVE

Sick leave without hospitalisation, shall be deductable from the Annual Leave. Leave must be recommendation by a registered medical practitioner appointed by the Company, or by a Government medical officer or in cases of emergency by other registered medical practitioner be entitled paid sick leave in each calendar year as follows:﻿

SPECIAL LEAVE

Representation the Company, State or National levels in any sports event & attended any social activities or any activities required by Law (Rukun Tetangga, Pasukan sukarela & etc).


EXAMINATION & STUDY LEAVE

Employee may be given leave to study or sit an examination which relevant to his current job or to the employee’s present or future assignment in the company. Examination leave is based on number of days for examination.
The company may gives : Full paid leave / Half paid leave / Unpaid leave


PROLONGED MEDICAL LEAVE

Any confirmed employee that recommendation by Company's panel doctors or Government medical officer, suffering from Tuberculosis, Cancer, Leukemia, Cerebral thrombosis, Poliomyelitis, Mental illness, AIDS, or any other prolonged illness with or without hospitalisation, may be granted paid medical leave as follow: 

3 months with full pay / 6 additional months at half-pay / 12 additional months with no pay
